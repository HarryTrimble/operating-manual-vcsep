Advice and support available through the support line

People in emergencies can call the British Red Cross support line to speak to a trained adviser for advice and support, including: 

  • Emotional support
  • How to get supplies like food and medication
  • Information to contact other services
  • Support to get specialist services such as mental health, finance and supplies
  • Support planning your next steps
