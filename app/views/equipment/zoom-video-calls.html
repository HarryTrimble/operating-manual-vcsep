Zoom video calls

Zoom is video calling service.

The support line uses Zoom video calls for:

  • Training operators
  • Briefing operators before shifts
  • Debriefing operators after shifts

How to join a Zoom meeting

For any meeting using Zoom video calls, we will send you either:

  • A link to a website
  • A code to enter into your computer. For example '342 423 098'

What device can I use Zoom on

You can use Zoom on any device connected to The Internet:

  • Desktop computer
  • Laptop computer
  • Tablet computer
  • Smart phone

How to download Zoom

You don't have to download Zoom to usen it.

Some people prefer to use the Zoom app, rather than the website.

Download the Zoom app for your device

Do I need a Zoom account?

You don't need Zoom account to join a support line video meeting.
