Aim of support line

Make it easier for people without a support network to talk to someone and get emotional support

Who for?

The support is for people having to self-isolate from Coronavirus (COVID-19):

  1. Most medically vulnerable people
  2. Other people needing to fully self-isolate for the expected period of 12 weeks

What help?

The support line offers primarily emotional support to people having to self-isolate from Coronavirus (COVID-19).

For some people calling we may need to give them other support we don't advertise, including:

  • How to get supplies like food and medication
  • Information to contact other services
  • Support to get specialist services such as mental health, finance and supplies
  • Support planning your next steps

When and how?

  • Starting date: 31 March 2020
  • Operating hours will be 10am – 6pm, Seven days a week
