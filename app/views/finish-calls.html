How to finish calls

Every call will be different. There is no one way to finish the call.

Check before you finish the call that you’ve covered:

  1. Introduction
  2. Caller’s name
  3. Do they have a support network
  4. Vulnerabilities they might have
  5. Support they need
  6. Consent to share information
  7. Address
  8. Contact details
  9. Confirm their next steps
  10. Say they can call again
